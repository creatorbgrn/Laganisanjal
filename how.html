The process

How LaganiSanjal works

A transparent, reviewed path that protects both businesses and investors.

For businesses & startups

1. Register your venture. Create an account and complete the business form: your idea, business plan, future plan, the funding amount you need in NRs and how you will use it. You can attach up to 8 product sample photos.

2. We review it. Every submission is checked before it goes public. You will see the status — pending, approved or rejected — in your dashboard.

3. Go live. Once approved, your listing appears on the public Browse page where investors can discover it.

4. Update anytime. Edit your listing from your dashboard. Edited listings are reviewed again before going live, so information stays accurate.

Register your business

For investors

1. Create your profile. Tell us your investor type, the industries and business stages you prefer, your region focus and the amount range you are ready to invest in NRs.

2. We review it. Investor profiles are reviewed to keep the platform trustworthy for businesses.

3. Browse opportunities. Explore approved businesses that match your interests and stage preferences.

4. Stay in control. Update your preferences from your dashboard whenever your investment focus changes.
