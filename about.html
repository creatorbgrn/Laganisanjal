About us

Building Nepal's bridge between ideas and capital.

We believe great businesses grow when good ideas meet the right backing.

Our mission

LaganiSanjal exists to make it easier for Nepali entrepreneurs and small businesses to find the funding they need to grow. Too many promising ventures never reach investors simply because there is no common place to meet. We are changing that.

What we do

We give startups and small businesses a structured way to present their idea, business plan and funding needs, and we give investors a curated view of opportunities that match their interests. Every listing is reviewed before it goes live, so the platform stays trustworthy for everyone.

Why Nepal

From agriculture and tourism to technology and handicrafts, Nepal is full of entrepreneurial energy. LaganiSanjal is built specifically for the Nepali market, with funding figures in NRs and categories that reflect the local economy.

Privacy first

Personal contact details are never published on public listings. Only the information needed to evaluate an opportunity is shown, keeping members in control of their data.

Registering and listing on LaganiSanjal is currently free for both businesses and investors.
